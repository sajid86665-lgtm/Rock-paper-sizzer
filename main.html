Rock, Paper, Scissors Game User Guide

Introduction:

Welcome to the Rock, Paper, Scissors game! This accessible game allows you to compete against the computer and test your luck and strategy. The goal is to reach a score of 10 points before the computer does.

Game Elements:

  • Buttons:

    There are three buttons representing your possible moves: Rock, Paper, and Scissors. These buttons are labeled appropriately for screen readers.

  • Scoreboard:

    The scoreboard displays your score and the computer's score. The first player to reach 10 points wins.

  • Reset Game Button:

    This button allows you to reset the game at any time.

  • Result Message:

    Just above the reset game button, there is a Result Message. This area provides feedback on the outcome of each round, such as who won or if it's a tie.

How to Play:

  1. Use your screen reader to navigate to the game elements.
  2. Choose your move by selecting one of the buttons labeled Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  3. After you make your choice, the computer will randomly select its move.
  4. The result of the round will be announced by the screen reader, and the scores will be updated. To earn a point, consider the following rules:
    • If you choose Rock and the computer chooses Scissors, you win the round.
    • If you choose Paper and the computer chooses Rock, you win the round.
    • If you choose Scissors and the computer chooses Paper, you win the round.
    • If both you and the computer choose the same thing, then that will be a tie, and no points will be given to both.
  5. Continue playing until one player reaches a score of 10.

Winning the Game:

  • If you reach a score of 10 before the computer, you will win! and you will hear a message for your victory.
  • If the computer reaches 10 points first, it wins, and you'll hear a message about the computer's victory.

  • Play Again Button:

    After a game is finished, this button appears to start a new game.

Resetting the Game:

  • If you want to start a new game or reset the current one, simply press the Reset Game button. You'll hear a sound to confirm the reset.

User Guide Toggle:

  • There is a "Show User Guide to play this game" button you can use to toggle the visibility of this guide.

Enjoy playing Rock, Paper, Scissors! Have fun testing your luck and strategy against the computer.
